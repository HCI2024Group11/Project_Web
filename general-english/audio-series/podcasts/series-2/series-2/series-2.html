Podcasts series 2

Learn everyday English by listening to our podcasts in series 2. All the episodes in the second series are divided into different sections, from fictional drama and jokes to quizzes and language advice.

You will improve your listening comprehension and develop your vocabulary. Each recording has a transcript and interactive exercises to help you understand and use the language.
