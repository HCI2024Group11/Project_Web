Audio zone

Do you want to listen to engaging and interesting audio recordings on a wide range of topics?

In this section, listen to audio recordings of people talking naturally about topics that interest them. The speakers are from all over the world, so you will hear a wide range of accents. There are audios for learners at B2 English level (upper intermediate) and C1 English level (advanced).

You will improve your listening comprehension and develop your vocabulary. Each recording has a transcript and interactive exercises to help you understand and use the language.
